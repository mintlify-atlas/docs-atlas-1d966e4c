The chef dashboard is your main hub after logging in. It loads your full profile, your complete recipe catalog, and your active subscriber list in a single view.

## Profile section

At the top of the dashboard, your profile card displays:

- **Name** — your chef name as registered
- **Description** — a short bio visible to subscribers (editable)
- **Photo** — loaded from your profile photo URL (editable)
- **Star rating** — your current rating out of 5 stars (editable)

Click **Editar Perfil** to update your description, photo URL, or star rating. Click **Guardar** to save, or **Cancelar** to discard changes.

## Your recipe catalog

The main section of the dashboard lists all your published recipes. Each recipe card shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Title** | The recipe's public title |
| **Subtitle** | A short tagline |
| **Description** | A brief public description (plaintext) |
| **Category** | The cooking category (e.g., Mexicana, Italiana) |
| **Difficulty** | Fácil, Media, or Difícil |
| **Prep time** | Estimated preparation time |
| **Fans** | Number of subscribers who have favorited the recipe |

<Info>
The actual recipe content — ingredients, preparation steps, and images — is encrypted and stored in the secure vault. It is not displayed on the dashboard card.
</Info>

### Quick actions per recipe

From each recipe card you can:

- **Editar** — fetch and decrypt the recipe content into an edit form, make changes, then re-encrypt and save
- **Borrar** — permanently delete the recipe record and its encrypted file from the vault

## Active subscribers

The right panel lists every subscriber who currently has an active contract to your content. For each subscriber you can see:

- **Name**
- **Email**
- **Contract expiry date** (shown as `Vence: <date>`)

Subscribers whose contracts have expired do not appear in this list.

## Quick actions from the dashboard

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Upload a recipe" icon="upload">
    Click **+ Nueva Receta** to open the recipe form. Fill in metadata and encrypted content, then submit to encrypt and publish.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit a recipe" icon="pen-to-square">
    Click **Editar** on any recipe card. The platform decrypts the content for you to modify, then re-encrypts on save.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Delete a recipe" icon="trash">
    Click **Borrar** on any recipe card. Both the database record and the `.enc` vault file are removed permanently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Update your profile" icon="user">
    Click **Editar Perfil** in the profile section to update your description, photo URL, or star rating.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
