## What makes a chef account different

A chef account gives you publishing capabilities that a subscriber account does not. As a chef, you can:

- Upload, edit, and delete encrypted recipes in your personal vault
- View the list of subscribers who have an active contract to your content
- Manage your public profile, including your description, photo, and star rating

Subscriber accounts can only browse and read recipes they have subscribed to — they cannot publish content.

## What happens during registration

When you register as a chef, the platform does more than create an account. It generates a cryptographic identity for you:

1. An **ECDSA P-256 key pair** is generated automatically via a secure Python process.
2. Your **private key is encrypted** using a key derived from your password, then stored in the database alongside the encryption salt and nonce.
3. Your **public key** is stored in plaintext and is used to verify your identity.
4. A unique **32-byte confirmation token** is generated and sent to your email address.

Your private key never leaves the server unencrypted. When you log in, the platform decrypts it in memory using your password to verify cryptographic integrity.

<Warning>
Your private key is derived from your password. If you forget your password, your cryptographic identity **cannot be recovered**. Choose a strong, memorable password before registering.
</Warning>

## Registration steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the registration page">
    Open the app and click **Register as Chef** on the authentication page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your full name, email address, and a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Register**. The platform generates your ECDSA P-256 key pair automatically — no action is required from you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation email from Bóveda Culinaria. Click the **Activar Cuenta de Chef** link inside.

    <Note>
      The confirmation link contains a unique 32-byte token. You must click it before you can log in. The link is single-use and tied to your account.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Return to the app, enter your email and password, and click **Login**. The platform verifies your password, decrypts your private key in memory to confirm cryptographic integrity, and redirects you to your chef dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After registration

Once you log in for the first time, you are taken directly to your [chef dashboard](/chef/dashboard). From there you can upload your first recipe, set up your profile, and start building your subscriber base.
