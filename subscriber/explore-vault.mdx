The recipe vault is the central catalog of all recipes published on the platform. Every recipe from every chef is listed here. You can browse and filter the vault freely — no subscription required to see the listings.

## What the vault shows

Each recipe card in the vault displays the following information:

- **Title** and subtitle
- **Description** — a short overview of the dish
- **Chef name** — who published the recipe
- **Category** — the cooking style (e.g., Mexicana, Italiana, Japonesa)
- **Difficulty** — Fácil, Media, or Difícil
- **Preparation time** — estimated time in minutes
- **Favorite count** — how many subscribers have saved this recipe

<Info>
  The actual recipe content — ingredients, preparation steps, and images — is always encrypted. You only see that content after you open a recipe with an active subscription.
</Info>

## Searching the vault

Use the search bar at the top of the vault to find recipes by:

- Recipe title
- Chef name
- Recipe description

Typing in the search bar filters the list in real time.

## Filtering results

You can narrow the vault down using the available filters:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Category">
    Filter by cooking category. Select a category such as Mexicana, Italiana, or Japonesa to show only recipes in that style.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Difficulty">
    Filter by difficulty level: **Fácil**, **Media**, or **Difícil**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Max preparation time">
    Set a maximum preparation time in minutes. Only recipes that take that long or less are shown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sort order">
    Sort the results by:
    - **Newest first** (`fecha_creacion`) — most recently published recipes appear at the top
    - **Most popular** (`valoracion`) — recipes with the most favorites appear at the top
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What's encrypted vs what's visible

| Field | Visible in the vault |
|---|---|
| Title, subtitle, description | Yes |
| Chef name, category, difficulty | Yes |
| Preparation time, favorite count | Yes |
| Ingredients | No — encrypted |
| Preparation steps | No — encrypted |
| Recipe images | No — encrypted |

<Tip>
  Browse the vault before committing to a subscription. You can read the description, check the difficulty, and see how popular a recipe is — all without subscribing. This helps you decide which chefs are worth subscribing to.
</Tip>

## Recipes you're not subscribed to

If you click on a recipe from a chef you haven't subscribed to, the platform prompts you to subscribe to that chef before showing the encrypted content. See [Subscriptions](/subscriber/subscriptions) for details on how to subscribe.
