---
title: "Subscriber Registration"
description: "Create a subscriber account to access encrypted chef recipes"
---

A subscriber account gives you access to the RDCS platform as a reader and collector of culinary content. Once registered, you can:

- Browse the full recipe vault across all chefs
- Subscribe to individual chefs to unlock their encrypted recipes
- Save recipes to your personal cookbook (favorites)
- Access encrypted ingredients, preparation steps, and images

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Navigate to the registration page and select **Register as Subscriber**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your full name, email address, and a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Submit the form. The platform automatically generates your cryptographic identity — an ECDSA P-256 key pair — in the background. You do not need to do anything to trigger this.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Log in with your email and password. You land directly on your subscriber dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Your private key is encrypted with your password. If you forget your password, your cryptographic identity cannot be recovered. There is no password reset that preserves your key.
</Warning>

## Your subscriber dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard is divided into three main areas:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Recipe vault" icon="vault">
    The full catalog of recipes from all chefs on the platform. Search and filter freely, even before subscribing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="My subscriptions" icon="file-contract">
    Your active chef contracts, including start and expiry dates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="My cookbook" icon="book-open">
    Your personal collection of favorited recipes for quick access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
