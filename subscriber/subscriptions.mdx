---
title: "Subscriptions"
description: "Subscribe to a chef and manage your active contracts"
---

A subscription gives you access to all encrypted recipes from a specific chef for the duration of your contract. Each subscription is a contract between you and a chef — it has a fixed start date, an end date, and a status.

## Subscription periods

When subscribing, you choose one of three contract lengths: **3 months**, **6 months**, or **12 months**. These are the only valid periods — the database schema enforces this with a `CHECK` constraint.

<Note>
  Pricing is set by each chef and passed as the `costo` field when creating a subscription. There are no platform-defined fixed prices.
</Note>

## How to subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a chef">
    Locate a chef whose recipes you want to access. You can find chefs through the vault by browsing or searching recipes, or by clicking on a recipe that interests you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Subscribe">
    On a recipe that belongs to an unsubscribed chef, click **Desbloquear Contenido**. The subscription modal opens showing the available periods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a period">
    Select your preferred subscription length: 3, 6, or 12 months.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Enter a 16-digit card number in the payment field. The platform validates that the card number has at least 16 digits before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirmar Pago Simulado**. A contract is created automatically with today as the start date and the expiry date calculated from your chosen period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your subscription becomes active immediately. You can now open and read all encrypted recipes from that chef.

<Note>
  The payment flow is a simulation. In the current version, any 16-digit card number is accepted. No real payment is processed.
</Note>

## Viewing your subscriptions

Go to **My Library** to see all your active subscriptions. Each entry shows:

- Chef name and photo
- Contract start date (`fecha_inicio`)
- Contract expiry date (`fecha_fin`)
- Contract status (`activo`)

## When a subscription expires

Once your contract's end date passes, the platform automatically denies all decryption requests for that chef's recipes. When you try to open a recipe, you will see an access denied message instead of the content.

<Warning>
  There is no auto-renewal. When your subscription expires, you must manually subscribe again to regain access to that chef's recipes.
</Warning>
